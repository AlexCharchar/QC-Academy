High School Program

Duration: 10 weeks

Enroll Now

A transformative opportunity for high school students to explore the world of barbering through hands-on mentorship and real-world experience.

Course Overview

Quality Cuts Barber Academy proudly presents an exclusive opportunity for high school students with a passion for the art of barbering. Our specialized beginner's course offers an immersive, hands-on experience designed to cultivate essential skills and instill a strong work ethic that will serve them well into the future.

This unique course, offered at a discounted rate, aims to empower young adults to explore their interests while gaining invaluable practical knowledge. Hosted at Quality Cuts Barbershop in the vibrant West Island community, students will embark on a 10-week journey under the guidance of our dedicated team of educators, each deeply committed to nurturing students' talents and fostering growth.

Structured into two distinct sections, the course provides a comprehensive learning experience. The initial five weeks focus on theoretical teachings, laying a solid groundwork for understanding the intricate artistry and business dynamics of barbering. Following this, the subsequent five weeks are dedicated to hands-on practical sessions, where students hone their skills on mannequins and real clients they bring in.

Throughout the course, students will delve into the fundamentals of barbering, mastering various techniques tailored to diverse hair textures. From classic cuts to contemporary styles, they will gain proficiency in executing precision haircuts with finesse. Additionally, they will receive invaluable insights into marketing strategies, empowering them to confidently attract and retain clients.

Ideal for creatively inclined individuals, this course offers a gateway to a fulfilling career in the dynamic world of barbering. Join us at Quality Cuts Barber Academy and embark on a transformative journey towards realizing your full potential as a skilled and confident barber.

Our 10-week high school barbering program is a perfect addition to your school's extracurricular offerings. We welcome collaboration with high schools interested in sponsoring students and covering associated expenses. If your school isn't yet offering this exciting opportunity, we invite you to connect with us to explore the possibilities of establishing a collaborative partnership. Together, we can empower young talents to embark on a rewarding journey in the art of barbering.


Ideal For

High school students with an interest in barbering, creativity, or hands-on careers. Perfect for teens looking to explore a professional path, gain real-world skills, and experience the discipline and pride of barber culture in a supportive environment.


Special Note

This highly successful program was featured on CBC News and is trusted by schools across the region. We actively welcome partnerships with high schools interested in sponsoring students or incorporating this course into their extracurricular offerings.
