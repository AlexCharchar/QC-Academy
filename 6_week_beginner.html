Beginner Barber Training

Duration: 6 weeks

C$20.00

Enroll Now

A complete hands-on beginner program designed to build a strong foundation in barbering — from theory to practical skills — guided by expert instructors every step of the way.

Course Overview

Embark on a transformative journey with our comprehensive 6-week Beginner Training Course. Delve deep into the art of barbering with three days of instructional training per week, each session spanning four hours of immersive learning.

Ideal for those craving a thorough understanding and supervised hands-on experience, this course is tailored to provide a solid foundation in barbering techniques. From mastering essential theory to practical application, participants will undergo a dynamic learning experience designed to elevate their skills to new heights.

In the latter half of the course, emphasis shifts to practical application as participants hone their newfound skills on mannequins and real clients they bring in. Plus, each participant will receive a scissor and clipper barber starter kit, ensuring they're equipped for success from day one.

Upon completion, graduates will be awarded a certificate recognized and respected within the barber community, marking the beginning of a promising career in barbering. Don't miss this unparalleled opportunity to kickstart your journey toward barbering excellence.


What You’ll Learn

  • Core barbering theory: sanitation, tools, client consultation
  • Clipper control, scissor work, sectioning, and fading fundamentals
  • Beard shaping, line-ups, and basic styling techniques
  • Confidence behind the chair through guided hands-on practice
  • Proper workflow and etiquette in a professional barbershop setting

Included in the Course

Eighteen 4-hour training sessions over six weeks, personalized instruction, supervised practice on mannequins and real clients, certificate of completion, and a professional barber starter kit (scissors and clippers).


Ideal For

Absolute beginners with little to no prior experience who are serious about starting a professional career in barbering and want a structured, supportive learning environment.
