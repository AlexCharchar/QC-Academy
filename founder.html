Meet Our Founder & Educator

What we offer

William Biney

Meet William Biney, the passionate owner of Quality Cuts Salon, which has been serving the community since 2010. With over two decades of experience in the barbering industry, William's love for the craft is evident in every aspect of his work.

Before establishing his own salon, William honed his skills as a senior barber, gaining invaluable experience along the way. He has a fervent desire to share his extensive knowledge and expertise with aspiring barbers, which led him to become a dedicated educator.

Throughout his career, William has actively participated in workshops and training sessions, including those conducted by renowned industry experts like Josh Lamonaca (MENSPIRE), Peter Gosling (Glassbox), and Alex Michael (SilverArrow).

As an ambassador and educator for the Elegance product line, William has been instrumental in shaping the careers of many successful barbers.

Not only is William committed to excellence in barbering, but he also represents the entrepreneurial spirit as an ambassador for the RW&CO clothing line. His talents have even graced the film industry, where he worked alongside celebrities like Angela Bassett and Mary J. Blige on the set of "Betty & Coretta."

What sets William apart is his innate gift for teaching. Patient and dedicated, he ensures that each student truly comprehends and masters the teachings, fostering a supportive learning environment where everyone can thrive.

Join William & his experienced team of educators on a journey of barbering excellence and unlock your fullest potential under his expert guidance.
