About Us

QC Barber Academy is a leading barbering school in Montreal, dedicated to helping students master the art and business of barbering. Based in Pierrefonds in the West Island, our programs offer in-depth training that goes far beyond the basics.


Led by industry professionals, our courses blend technical skills — like clipper work, fading, and scissor techniques—with real-world knowledge in client care, marketing, and self-branding. You’ll learn not only how to cut hair with precision, but how to build a successful barbering career from the ground up.


Our training includes beginner, intermediate, and advanced barber courses, all taught in an authentic barbershop setting. Whether you're a high school student looking to get started or a working barber wanting to refine your skills, we have a program for you.


Students train with real clients, receive ongoing feedback from skilled educators, and graduate with the confidence and ability to thrive in the industry.


If you’re searching for a top-rated barber school in Montreal, or want to join a barber academy in the West Island with a proven track record — QC Barber Academy is your destination.
